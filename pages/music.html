My Music!

Here is a collection of all the music I've made and published. A majority was made using Garageband, with occacional edits using Audacity. Some tracks on Sounds of the Dreamscape use Creative Commons audio samples for ambience. I'm currently working on a new album called Arcane Computer, most similar to Sounds of the Dreamscape and Pink Goop.

Note: To get my music on Spotify, I had to publish it through a distributor, and I'm not happy with the audio quality. I would recommend listening on one of the other platforms linked.
